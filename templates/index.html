Hand Gesture Based Virtual Application

Hand Gesture Based Virtual Application enables users to interact with their computer using simple hand movements. This technology provides a seamless and intuitive way to control your system without the need for physical devices. Whether you're navigating, selecting options, or scrolling, it offers a hands-free and efficient experience.
